Dashboard Overview

Noom dDPP Activity Dashboard: An overview of patient weekly engagement in health behavior change activities via the digital diabetes prevention (Noom) Noom app and using activity trackers. Patient self-reported data includes Physical Activities, Digital Learning, Weigh-ins & In-app Communication (with personal coach & support group).

Noom: a diabetes prevention & weight loss behavior mobile application based on the CDC’s evidence-based diabetes prevention program (DPP) curriculum. This 52-week program allows users to log their meals, physical activities, weight, and provides daily articles covering heath behavior topics while connecting them with a support group, and a behavior-change coach

Notes: this dashboard is part of an IRB-approved research study. Not all of your patients will have an active dashboard in the EHR

How to Navigate Dashboard

Hover over the text in the following definitions to see their associated dashboard sections.

Patient’s Self-logged Noom Activity Data: patient’s self-reported activity data & health indicators as part of dDPP program, including those on Noom app

Icon: Click-on icon for historical data

Weekly Engagement Progress: weekly High/Low engagement across 4 Noom features over 52 weeks

Current Week’s Engagement Level: patient’s overall engagement score for the current week.
