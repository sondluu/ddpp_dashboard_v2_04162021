Dashboard Overview

Noom DDPP Activity Dashboard: summarizes patient’s engagement in diabetes prevention health behavior change via Noom app while using activity tracking devices, over 52-weeks. Weekly self-logged data covers: Physical Activities, Digital Learning, Weigh-ins & In-app Communication (w/ coach & support group).

Noom: Health behavior change mobile application based on the CDC’s DPP curriculum that allows users to log their meals, physical activity minutes, weight, daily health articles while connecting them with a support group, and a behavior-change coach

Navigate Dashboard

Icon: Click-on icon for historical data
